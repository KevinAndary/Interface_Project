Al Mirqab Al Jadeed

Interface is the provider for the signage and wayfinding solutions for the Al Mirqab Al Jadeed Compound, a premier residential community located in the heart of Al Sadd, Qatar. The compound is a family-oriented development featuring four multi-story residential buildings, a shared pool, and a state-of-the-art gym.

Our team was tasked with creating an effective and aesthetically pleasing wayfinding system that ensures ease of navigation for all residents and visitors, while also enhancing the overall experience of living in this vibrant community. The signage was designed to be both functional and in line with the modern, upscale look of the compound.
